About Nova Hospital

Leading the way in medical excellence since 2010

Our Story

Nova Hospital was founded with a single mission: to provide world-class healthcare with a human touch. Located in Balikesir, we have grown from a small clinic to a leading multi-specialty hospital, serving thousands of patients every year with dedication and advanced medical technology.

Our Mission

To improve the health and well-being of our community through compassionate care and medical innovation.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence and patient safety.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency.
  • Compassion: We treat every patient like family.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
